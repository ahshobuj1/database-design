Multi-Tenant SaaS DJ Platform
Software Requirements & Feature Analysis Document
1. Project Overview
Project Name

Multi-Tenant SaaS DJ Platform

Project Type

Software as a Service (SaaS) Web Application

Business Goal

This platform allows DJs to create and manage their own professional portfolio websites, receive booking requests, collect payments, and manage events through a subscription-based SaaS platform.

Each DJ will have:

Their own dashboard
Subscription management
Customizable portfolio website
Booking management
Payment collection
Invoice tracking

The SaaS owner (Super Admin) will manage:

All users
Subscription plans
Payments
Themes
Invoices
Platform settings
2. Core Business Concept

The platform works as follows:

A DJ visits the main landing page.
The DJ registers and creates an account.
The DJ subscribes to a paid plan via Stripe.
After subscribing, the DJ accesses a private dashboard.
The DJ selects one of the available portfolio themes.
The DJ customizes the selected theme.
The DJ adds mixtapes and events.
The DJ publishes their portfolio website.
The portfolio becomes live on a unique subdomain.
Clients visit the portfolio and submit booking requests.
The DJ manages booking requests.
The DJ sends payment links to clients.
Clients pay online or choose cash payment.
The DJ tracks invoices and payment statuses.
